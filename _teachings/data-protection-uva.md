I gave a guest lecture on March 18, 2026 in the Master's-level Data Protection Technologies course at the University of Amsterdam, coordinated by dr. Francesco Regazzoni. The course is part of the joint Master's in Computer Science and covers fundamental data protection technologies, including advanced cryptographic techniques, differential privacy, anonymization, and trusted execution environments. My lecture was based on my paper [*Fundamental Limitations of Favorable Privacy-Utility Guarantees for DP-SGD*](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10237), accepted at ACM CCS 2026.
